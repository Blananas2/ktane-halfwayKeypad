On the Subject of Halfway Keypad

"One-fourth of the way through a Keypad" doesn’t sound nearly as catchy.

  • Find the pressed symbol alongside the diagonal arrow corresponding to the position of the pressed button in the grid below.
  • Press the remaining buttons in the order their symbols appear along the direction of the arrow. When you reach the edge, change the angle such that the angle of incidence equals the angle of reflection.
  • Warning: Arrows are not shown below. However their directions can be deduced, refer to "Deducing Arrow Directions".

Deducing Arrow Directions

The following is what is known about the arrow directions on the previous page:

  • Each symbol occurs exactly four times, with each one’s arrow pointing in a different direction.
  • Arrows will never point such that there are less than three symbols along that direction.
  • The process of elimination and the two above properties can always be used to find the direction for a specific symbol.
